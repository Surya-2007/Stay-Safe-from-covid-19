HOW TO STAY SAFE FROM COVID 19

-Made with Love by ProGrad Junior,Surya Adusumilli

Wash Your Hands Regularly

Regular Hand wash with soap or sanitizer every 30 minutes or so

Cover Your mouth & nose

Cover your mouth and nose while sneezing or coughing in public.Even better use a mask.

Stay home Stay Safe

Avoid travelling or being in crowded place. Stay Indoor.

Eat Healthy

Avoid consuming junk food eat as healthy as possible to boost immunity

Stay calm

Regular excersise or meditation is a great way to keep yourself calm and composed
